How to Play: Chess

For this game, 2 Players on your device (Local Play only. Wireless Play and Versus AI is not included).

Making a Move:

1. On your turn, Select your chess piece and click on the square to move.

2. Illegal moves are not valid and will be ignored.

Game Rules

1. The game follows the standard chess rules (e.g. pawn promotion, en passant, check and checkmate detection).

2. The game enforced the turn-based logic, white always starts first on first turn.
